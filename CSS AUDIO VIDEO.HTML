XTRAORDINARY GIRLS

"Hibernation Deactivated, Landing Sequence Initiated"

 

The group name "XG" stands for "Xtraordinary Girls," and they aim to empower people in various situations around the world through their unconventional style of music and performance. XG is a hip-hop/R&B-inspired girl group comprised of 7 members: JURIN, CHISA, HARVEY, HINATA, JURIA, MAYA, and COCONA.

 

They debuted in March 2022 with their first single ‘TIPPY TOES’ under the global entertainment production company XGALX, which promotes a bold culture and produces artist groups with unique worldviews. Their second single ‘MASCARA’ was released in June of the same year. On January 25th 2023, they released their 3rd single, ‘SHOOTING STAR’ which included two tracks, ‘SHOOTING STAR’ and ‘LEFT RIGHT’. On September 27, 2023, XG’s 1st Mini Album, 'NEW DNA' was released.

 

Their discography is something worth the listen, with songs that sonically speaks for the girl group. Being able to step out of their comfort zone and not be held back by the customs of the norms in their music and style. XG’s songs have this catchiness and such out of this world themes that goes along with it.

COSMIC VISUAL ELEMENT

"Extraordinary Orbital Elements"


Apart from the songs that XG puts out, they also hold strong visuals and a taste for authentic and well-structured fashion ensembles. Experimentation and thoughtful inspirations from established styles comes into play. It is quite the break from the ordinary and the explosion of unique and interesting themes accompanied by their songs. It would always make the ALPHAZ feel the urge to want more as they could not get enough of the visuals that are put out by the girl group.

 

With each member of the band, such well-rounded and truly extraordinary personalities can be found within each and every member. This personality can be very much seen in the way the girls embody their music through their music videos and even on live performances. There is an essence of candid and genuine energy that bursts on every member visible through the way they perform their songs.

 

There is an evident refreshing tone to the girl group that every song and the visuals that comes along with it has interesting aspects to it that makes them stand out. Never settling for virality, but for the genuine and authentic creative works that the girl group creates. Much of this authenticity is through the charisma and uniqueness of each and every member of the band.

 

Below are the creative visuals of XG’s music projects. Much of these music videos have the sense of being out of this world aligned to the supposed lore of the girl group being space girls. Experimentation on interesting themes accompanied by intricate fashion ensembles that goes along with the visual route that the era is going for.
